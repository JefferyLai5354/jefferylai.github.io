Club History

The club was established on September 25, 2018. It originated as small gatherings of eco-friendly volunteers to share and discuss environmental perspectives.

Originally, the club was located in the New Territories and later moved to the Central Building.

By 2019, the total number of members had reached 800. Since then, the membership has grown to about 2,000, of which about 90% are national volunteers and 10% are international volunteers, i.e. foreigners.
