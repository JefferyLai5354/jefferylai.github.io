Mission Statement

We are a national social organization with active participation, support and voluntary application by volunteers. Based on the basic idea of publicizing environmental protection and promoting ecological balance, establish a platform for the exchange and docking of the latest ecological and environmental protection ideas and consultations, so that more people can establish environmental protection concepts and join the team that protects the natural environment and promotes the development of green life.

    In achieving our Mission we will:

  • Maintain the original intention of promoting environmental protection and promoting ecological balance.
  • Maintain a Club environment which isopen and free speech.
  • Provide quality services and products at a fair value.
  • Provide lectures and activities on environmental topics.
  • Listen to members' demands and adopt members' suggestions actively.
